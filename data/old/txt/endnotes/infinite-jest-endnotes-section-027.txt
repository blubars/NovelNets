

27
   Metro Boston subdialectical argot -- origin unknown -- for cannabis, pot, grass, du-Bois, dope, ganja, bhang, herb, hash, m. jane, kif, etc.; with
'Bing Crosby' designating cocaine and organic methoxies ('drines), and -- inexplicably -- 'Doris' standing for synthetic dickies, psychs, and phenyls.
