

     She'd come out of the St. E.'s doors and turned right for the quick walk back up to Ennet and a grotesquely huge woman
whose hose bulged with stubble and whose face and head were four times larger than the largest woman Joelle had ever seen had
grabbed her arm at the elbow and said she was sorry to be the one to tell her but that unbeknownst to her she was in almost mind-
boggling danger.
     It took rather a while for Joelle to look her up and down. 'This is supposed to be news?'
